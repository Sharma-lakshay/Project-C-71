About Us

The Chairman: Mr. T.P. Aggarwal

The Chairman: Mr. T.P.Aggarwal

The school is run by St. Mark’s Christian Educational Society. This Society runs St. Mark’s Sr. Sec. Public School, Janakpuri & St. Mark’s Girls Sr. Sec. School, Meera Bagh too. The founder Chairman of the school Mr. T. P. Aggarwal has been working in the field of education since 1975. He has been awarded the Dr. Radhakrishnan Memorial National Teachers Award.

Mr. T. P. Aggarwal, Chairman of Group of St. Mark's School, Charter President of Rotary Club of Delhi, Janak (RI. District 3010). He is the President of IMPPA (Indian Motion Picture Producers Association), Chamber of Motion Picture Producers and also the Chairman of Film Makers Combine for the second Consecutive term. Mr. T. P. Aggarwal believes that every student should have a passion to work hard. Students should also respect elders, especially teachers, because they are like parents. Mr. T. P. Aggarwal has always laid great stress on co-curricular activities like games and sports and the importance of a healthy mind in a healthy body.

A philanthropist, with a secular outlook, Mr. Aggarwal believes in donating to temples, gurudwaras and churches alike. His maxim in life is that everyone should be acknowledged and rewarded for good work done. He does not belong to any particular political party, but continually works for the benefit and upliftment of humanity.

Manager: Mr. Rahul Aggarwal

Manager: Mr. Rahul Aggarwal

Mr. Rahul Aggarwal has a degree in media studies from Deakin University, Australia with theatre being his field of specialization.
A good communicator, flexible thinker and innovative practitioner, Mr. Rahul Aggarwal is quick to understand,accept and mould himself according to the emergent trends in the field of education.

Mr. Rahul Aggarwal believes in developing and maintaining positive and effective relations with schools both domestic as well as international.

A leader who focusses on successful outcomes for students,Mr.Rahul Aggarwal believes in the usage of technology in classroom teaching combined with the experience of well trained teachers.

Recipient of the Human Rights Brotherhood Award by All India Human Rights Association, Mr. Rahul Aggarwal believes in walking that extra mile to promote world peace.

Princpal: Ms. Alka Kher

Principal: Ms. Alka Kher

MS. ALKA KHER joined the school in 1983 dreaming of evolving the institution in such a way that students feel elated, nurtured and blessed to attend school everyday. She has always worked towards holistic and intellectual development of students. Her hard work and perseverance led to her promotion as Head Mistress in 1996; then as a Vice Principal in 2007 and now as a Principal to lead the school to name, fame and glory. Being a science hand, she has always believed in articulate planning, scientific and meticulous rendition of any task at hand. As a pro in History and English Literature, she takes keen interest in planning and organizing different educational and co-curricular activities. She spearheaded the CCE pattern of Examination System in school. With immense patience and forthright vision she succeeded in moulding the teachers and students and thus made the CCE a grand success in the school. She was selected by CBSE to mentor and guide other schools about the smooth functioning of CCE system. Students see a friend, philosopher and a guide in her and she implants dreams of success and pioneering leadership.

She believes in working as a TEAM, staying positive, working hard and making it happen.

She inspires all to believe in the quote – “Your mind is a powerful entity. When you fill it with positive thoughts, your life will start to change”.

Vice Principal: Ms. Inderpreet Kaur Ahluwalia

Vice Principal: Ms. Inderpreet Kaur Ahluwalia

Ms. Inderpreet Kaur Ahluwalia is a conscientious and compassionate educator with a sanguine personality. She is open-minded with a sound and optimist outlook for all things in life and possesses an excellent ability to motivate others. In her 24 years of experience in the field of education, Ms. Inderpreet has always demonstrated energy, creativity, and talent and has an eye for innovation in whatever she undertakes. She is an avid learner who keeps herself updated with the latest and wishes to impart and share her knowledge and experience with everyone. To this end, she has travelled extensively around the world for numerous conferences and exchange programmes and has a pivotal role in many global online projects.

Before joining St. Mark’s Janakpuri she had served in various prestigious schools all over the country and also as the Principal in Assam Rifles High School, Kohima, Nagaland. She firmly believes in child-centered education and feels that personality development and confidence building are the important facets of education. According to her, young minds can be motivated to dream big, aspire and blossom into good human beings with the love, trust, and faith of the teachers.
